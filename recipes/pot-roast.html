Mississippi Pot Roast

Description

This recipe features a delicious slowcooked entree known as the "Mississippi Pot Roast", a famous recipe used by slowcooker chefs across the Midwest. It turns a cut of chuck into a juicy, forktender meal that can feed the whole family! Serve the meat in sandwiches or simply serve it over rice! Keep in mind that because this is a slow cooker recipe, you will need at least 8 hours to fully cook this dish to perfection!

Ingredients

  • 21/2 lbs of boneless chuck roast
  • 1 jar (16 fl oz.) of pepperoncinis
  • 1/2 packet of Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1/2 packet of au jus gravy mix

Steps

  1. Place the chuck roast at the bottom of your slowcooker.
  2. Pour the packets of ranch dressing mix and au jus over the meat evenly.
  3. Pour half of the brine in the pepperoncinis jar over the meat, and top the meat with half of the pepperoncinis in the jar.
  4. Lid the slowcooker and set it to low.
  5. After 8 hours, shred the meat with a pair of forks and serve!
  6. Enjoy the meal!
