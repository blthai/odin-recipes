Mayo Ramen

Description

This recipe features a trendy instant ramen modification. This simple ramen hack elevates the flavor of instant ramen, and makes it comparable to ramen you may be served at a restaurant. It is easy to make and extremely quick to put together. Expect this recipe to take ten minutes at the most.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 500 mL of water

Steps

  1. Pour the water into a pot and get it to boiling on high heat.
  2. While the water raises to a boil, mince the garlic cloves and add to a bowl.
  3. Crack the raw egg into the bowl with the garlic and add in the ramen seasoning packet to the bowl.
  4. Once the water is boiling, cut the heat and add in the ramen noodles.
  5. Allow the ramen to cook for 21/2minutes.
  6. Pour half of the water from the pot into the bowl containing the mixture and stir thoroughly.
  7. Pour the rest of the ramen and water into the bowl and serve.
  8. Enjoy the meal!
